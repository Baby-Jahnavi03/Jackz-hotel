Hotel service encompasses all the amenities, assistance, and experiences provided to guests during their stay, extending beyond just lodging to include a range of services that enhance comfort, convenience, and overall satisfaction. These services can be tangible, like room service and laundry, or intangible, such as customer service and ambiance.
The goal of hotel service is to create a positive and memorable experience for guests, encouraging loyalty and positive reviews.
